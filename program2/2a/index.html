Program Education Objectives (PEO's)

  • Graduates will demonstrate technical skills and leadership in their chosen fields by solving real-time problems using current techniques and tools.
  • Graduates will communicate effectively as individuals or team members in local and global environments.
  • Graduates will demonstrate lifelong learning through continuing education and professional development.
  • Graduates will provide viable and sustainable solutions within societal, professional, environmental, and ethical contexts.
